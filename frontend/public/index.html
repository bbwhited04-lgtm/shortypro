How it works

1) Upload

Drop in a long video or paste a link. Add optional notes: tone, target platform, hashtags.

2) We cut shorts

Clips are created and appear in your dashboard. Approve, rename, or request another cut.

3) Schedule & post

Pick times, attach captions, and publish across platforms from one place.

FAQ

Do you post for me automatically?

ShortyPro is built around an approval + scheduling flow. You decide what posts, where, and when.

Is this built on open-source code?

Yes: the UI uses open-source libraries (e.g., Tailwind). You can self-host the front-end and swap in your own backend endpoints.

Can I connect multiple brands?

Yes—use brand presets (logos, fonts, caption style) in the dashboard.
